We present: Shiva
A sustainable booking engine, powered by greentech where the real environmental impact of the hotel industry is measured and published in real time.

First, hotels are equipped with sensors that allow real-time measurement of their environmental impact and account the consumption of resources.

Shiva is a sustainable hotel booking engine. We inform possible guests of the real impact their stay has on the environment. So conscious guests are better informed and so they can choose a more positive and greener hotel for their stay.

Shiva helps hotels improve daily, by making their impact visible, more accountable and allow them to have a more positive impact on the environment.

Shiva curates a highly commoditized industry under a new booking criteria . One that is more than relevant today.

What problem are we solving?

No more Greenwashing in the Hotel industry..

Sometimes companies make consumers believe that they value and prioritize sustainability, when reality is far from it.

Shiva is a greentech that solves the Greenwashing problem by collecting data from sensors installed in hotels and publishing it in real time without any human intervention.

Shiva takes you from awareness to action.
